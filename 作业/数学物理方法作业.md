> \[
> \frac{1}{{{z^2}\left( {z - 1} \right)}} \hfill \\

>    = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}\left( {n + 1} \right){{\left( {z - 1} \right)}^{n - 1}}}  \hfill \\

(4)

> 在 $\abs{z}>1$ 内展开
> \[
> {e^{1/\left( {1 - z} \right)}} = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {\frac{1}{{n!{{\left( {1 - z} \right)}^n}}}}  \hfill \\
>    = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {\frac{1}{{n!}}\frac{1}{{{z^n}}}\frac{1}{{{{\left( {1/z - 1} \right)}^n}}}}  \hfill \\
>    = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}\frac{1}{{n!}}\frac{1}{{{z^n}}}\frac{1}{{{{\left( {1 - 1/z} \right)}^n}}}}  \hfill \\
>    = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}\frac{1}{{n!}}\frac{1}{{{z^n}}}{{\left( {\sum\limits_{k = 0}^\infty  {\frac{1}{{{z^k}}}} } \right)}^n}}  \hfill \\
>    = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^\infty  {{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}\frac{1}{{n!}}{{\left( {\sum\limits_{k = 1}^\infty  {\frac{1}{{{z^k}}}} } \right)}^n}}  \hfill \\
> \]
> 我们记 $f(n,k)$ 表示 ${\left( {\sum\limits_{k = 1}^\infty  {\frac{1}{{{z^k}}}} } \right)^n}$ 中 $z^{-k}$ 的系数，则有
> \[
> \left\{ \begin{aligned}
>   &f(0,k)=0\quad k>-1\\
>   &f\left( {1,k} \right) = 1\quad k > 0 \hfill \\
>   &f\left( {n,k} \right) = \sum\limits_{m = 1}^{k - n + 1} {f\left( {n - 1,k - m} \right)} \quad k > n - 1 \hfill \\ 
> \end{aligned}  \right.
> \]
> 因此，原则上可以递推求出
> \[
> \left\{ \begin{aligned}
>   &{e^{1/\left( {1 - z} \right)}} = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^{ - \infty } {{a_n}{z^{ - n}}}  \hfill \\
>   &{a_k} = \sum\limits_{n = 0}^k {{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}\frac{1}{{n!}}f\left( {n,k} \right)}  \hfill \\ 
> \end{aligned}  \right.
> \]
